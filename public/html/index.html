Experience Charleston can count on.

Thomas Pritchard's almost 24-year tenure for Charleston Water System is a testament to his commitment to community and to service. If re-elected, Thomas will proudly continue to fulfill his obligation to the City of Charleston, bringing with him a wealth of experience that will aid CWS in serving our growing area.

Balanced Development & Smart Growth

"In 24 years with CWS, I have encouraged smart growth within the Urban Growth Boundary to protect undeveloped areas such as John's Island and Wadmalaw Island. I believe that CWS has roles to play in both pushing back against the proliferation of septic systems and encouraging stricter enforcement of the existing septic systems, while keeping in check rampant development."

- Thomas Pritchard

Environment & Sea Level Change

" In my almost twenty-four years on the Board of CWS, I have placed environmental stewardship at the forefront of our priorities and the staff has made it a hallmark of the operations as well. To that end, CWS has, and will continue to invest heavily in infrastructure maintenance and upgrades geared towards preserving and protecting the environment and clean water."

- Thomas Pritchard

Sound Governance & Financial Excellence

"I am committed to providing the proven, responsible leadership that has been the hallmark of my service to the citizens of Charleston throughout my time on the CWS Board. I pride myself on finding consensus among the Board members on even the most difficult of issues. We have built a stable, consistent organization which lends itself to well-run board meetings and smooth operation of CWS."

- Thomas Pritchard
